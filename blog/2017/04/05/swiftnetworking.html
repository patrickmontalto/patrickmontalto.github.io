SwiftNetworking

I made a thing.

It’s a CocoaPod called SwiftNetworking.

Sometimes, Alamofire or AFNetworking is just a bit too heavy for some use cases. You may not want to burden your project with such large depedencies. Enter SwiftNetworking.

It provides a lightweight NetworkClient to build and send URLRequests in Swift. Fully tested too, of course!

Check the documentation and example over at the CocoaPod page.
